eRabates

Provides a 10% discount on charge prices for electric vehicle owners. Earn the discount by accumulating 100 points through visits to charging stations. A maximum of 500 points can be stored at any time. Discounts do not stack.




eBenefits

LCalculates estimated savings when using electric vehicles instead of fuel. Benchmark: 2018 Toyota Corolla performance over every 50 miles.

There's a button to send an email with relevant information about the savings.
